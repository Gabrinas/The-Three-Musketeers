The Three Musketeers

Author: Alexandre Dumas


This story titled The Three Musketeers is a fictitious story about three guards that were involved in the state and court affairs in Paris. The name of the three guards are Athos, Parthos, and Aramis. There is a boy named D'artagnan, who left (on horse) his family for Paris with the hope of joining the Three Musketeers.
